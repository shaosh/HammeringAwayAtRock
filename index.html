Hammering Away At Rock

When nothing seems to help, I go look at a stonecutter hammering away at his rock perhaps a hundred
times without as much as a crack showing in it. Yet at the hundred and first blow it will split in two,
and I know it was not that blow that did it, but all that had gone before.
